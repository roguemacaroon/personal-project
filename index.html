About Me

Experience in front-end development with a dynamic professional background.

As a junior developer, I've accumulated valuable experience in front-end development. I am based in my home community of Elsipogtog First Nation and am proud to be part of the growing number of Indigenous people in technology.

While I am now building a career in tech, I started my journey over 10 years ago in healthcare.

I have always been a person that is dedicated to following my passions. I merged my love of helping others with my desire to learn and started out in health care as a nurse and medical office assistant, this branched off to doula work and adult education as a perinatal educator. When my own health prevented me from physically helping others as a nurse, I knew it was time to begin to follow my other passions, art and technology.

Fueled by a relentless pursuit of knowledge, every project is a fusion of creativity, empathy, and technical expertise. My goals of personal enrichment and creating positive change remain steadfast.

Through an Internship with Indigenous Friends Association,web development emerged as one of my strongest undeniable passions. Learning skills such as HTML, CSS, and JS, while also collaborating seamlessly via platforms like GitHub, I've honed not just technical skills but also a knack for marrying creativity with strategic insight. My aspiration now revolves around evolving into a proficient full-stack developer.

I embody a hybrid skill set that combines artistic prowess with technical expertise and compassionate care. Throughout my journey, I've cultivated an empathetic, courteous, and dependable approach, while ensuring accountability in every role I take.
